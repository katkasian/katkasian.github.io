Scraping Tech News to Prepare for a Translation Exam

In an attempt to combine two of my passions - tech and languages - I decided to get qualified as an IT translator, taking this Japanese exam. One of the best ways to study translation is examining the same text in two languages in parallel. Not managing to find many IT-related parallel texts, I decided to build a simple scraper to look at how media outlets translate tech related article. Check out my Github repository for an example.
